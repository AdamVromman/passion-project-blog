Ooooooookay, time to get to where I want to be. Today I am doing what I have been wanting do for some time. SVG transformation. My first idea is to try and do it with motion, since they have a free svg transform function, whereas the one from GSAP is paid only.

Motion has an example on their [website](), which has an output looking like this:
![motion example](/getting-some-momentum/motion-path-morphing.gif)

The code is slightly different from what I need, but it shouldn't be too hard to adapt it. The main difference are that they have a full array of paths, and I only have two, and that theirs is animated indefinitely, whereas I want mine to be animated only when a certain action is done. For example, when a new date is loaded, or something like that.

Unfortuntaly, the results are absolutely not what I am looking for. No matter how much I try to make an eye open and close, it keeps looking wonky and very laggy, to the point that it is unusable. I will have to switch to GSAP, I think.
